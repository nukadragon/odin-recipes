Ice cream recipe

Ooey gooey goodness

Ingredients

  • Marshmallows, full sized
  • Chocolate bar that can be broken into squares big enough for the graham cracker.
  • Graham crackers broken into two equal squares.
Instructions
  1. Heat the marshmallow over a fire or in microwave until desired color or softness.
  2. Place one square of chocolate on one square of graham cracker.
  3. Place the marshmallow on top of the chocolate square.
  4. Top with the other half of the graham cracker.
  5. Squish and enjoy.
