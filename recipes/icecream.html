Ice cream recipe

A quick recipe for a summer classic.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups heavy cream
  • 1.5 cups whole milk
  • .75 cup cane sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon sea salt
Instructions
  1. Combine ingredients over low heat until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Chill for 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Remove the chilled icecream from the bowl and churn in ice cream maker per maker's instructions.
  4. Add toppings and serve.
